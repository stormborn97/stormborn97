What is Eon studio?

A creative digital studio with technological-equipped space consisting of individuals who are working on variety of digital projects such as designing website, an app or a mobile game.

Anyone who's interested working with us are welcomed, however to be a part of Eon's future you must bring something good on the table. We would love to work with talented people who are passionate about creativity and creating solutions for the world's problems.

What does Eon stands for?

Eon comes from the phrase "aeon" which means "an indefinite and very long period of time. So technically speaking, "eon" is not abbreviation but it is a noun.
