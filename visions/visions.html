  • The Stranger at my dream...

    Eyes stares the unknown.
    The light fading off a fragile stranger,
    Standing at a far foot of a rail bridge.
    Pointing at the darkness,
    Shaking, soiling and trembling in fear.
    Of that which is still not known to me,
    A stranger at my dream.
