How did i create this?

First of all, i want to say that am not a pro at this, am more like a beginner and as a way to maintain a constant learning, i decided to create a simple evolutionary project that will keep me in line.

So i just started modelling in blender and it was a bit challenging because i had little time due to ongoing studies, so i had to go for assets and then it helped me to speed up the process.

Despite having assets from the store, i personally modeled some of them using Blender software and have them imported into unity Engine. It was fun.

Once i had the assets ready it was the time for environment design which is the main idea behind the prototype and the most challenging to get the concept laid down.

Why Lowpoly art design?

I love lowpoly design, due to its minimalist feeling and an artistic taste of it. To be more precise having low poly made the project more doable and less headaches otherwise i would have to deal with details at length for all the models and the character.

By the way, i have a slow computer and it is not well designed for graphics and that was another limiting factor here. But am looking forward to solve that.

Having lowpoly project on a slow computer reduces processing time and this is a design strategy someone would go for especially someone like me who have just a year into computer graphics.

In a near future, i'm looking forward to be more resourceful and have a more defined base structure to create more interesting projects. THanks!
